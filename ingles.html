ANGLOPHONE CULTURE HUB

Grade 10°

Students explore the history, culture, and society of the UK and the USA, recognizing traditions, art, and diversity while improving oral and written skills.

Grade 11°

Students analyze Canada and Australia through historical, social, and cultural perspectives, contrasting them with local reality to foster critical thinking and cultural awareness.

Cultural Gallery

Images, videos, and short reviews about art, music, literature, and key symbols of Anglophone countries. A visual space to enjoy cultural diversity.

Voices of Venecia

Student blog and podcast with reflections, interviews, and oral productions about Anglophone culture. A chance to practice English and share ideas.

Time & Map Explorer

Interactive maps and timelines linking historical events, figures, and cultural movements from the UK, USA, Canada, and Australia. Learn geography and history dynamically.

Why Learn English with Us??

In the Upper Secondary program, the Anglophone Culture area focuses on practical tools for real and academic communication. From basic knowledge to functional use, students strengthen listening, reading, writing, and speaking skills while exploring cultural contexts.

Learning Goals

  • Understand and use basic English expressions in school and daily life.
  • Develop conversational skills on familiar cultural topics.
  • Foster critical thinking through reading texts in English.
  • Promote autonomy with digital tools for language and culture learning.
